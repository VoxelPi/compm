For real numbers $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$ and $n \in \mathbb{N} we have$

    \begin{equation}\label{binomial_id}
    (x+y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n}\binom{n}{k}x^{n-k}y^{k}
\end{equation}


Formular \eqref{binomial_id} is referred to as the \textbf{binomial identity}.
